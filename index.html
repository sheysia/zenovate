About Us

Built on a decade of healthcare data engineering

Zenovate LLC is a healthcare data and AI consulting firm based in the Washington, D.C. metro area. We specialize in helping healthcare organizations move from AI experimentation to production-grade deployment.

Our founder brings 10+ years of hands-on experience in enterprise healthcare data engineering — working with claims datasets at billion-row scale, building production pipelines, and architecting analytics platforms for one of the nation's largest health plans.

We combine rare cross-disciplinary depth — spanning data engineering, financial analysis, and applied AI — with the practical knowledge of how healthcare data actually works: the messy business logic, the edge cases in claims adjudication, the compliance requirements that generic AI consultants overlook.

Solutions

What we build

End-to-end AI solutions designed for the unique complexities of healthcare data.
🤖

AI Agents for Healthcare Operations

Conversational AI agents that handle member inquiries, benefits verification, claims status lookups, and prior authorization workflows — replacing rigid IVR systems with intelligent, context-aware interactions.

📋

Intelligent Claims Processing

AI-assisted claim filing tools that guide providers through complex submissions, auto-validate against payer rules, flag potential denials before submission, and reduce rework cycles.

🔄

Data Platform Modernization

End-to-end migration of legacy healthcare data systems — SQL Server, SAS, on-prem warehouses — to modern cloud-native platforms like Databricks, with AI-assisted code translation and pipeline development.

📊

Claims Analytics & Data Quality

Automated data quality monitoring, anomaly detection, and claims analytics dashboards built on top of your existing data infrastructure — turning billions of rows into actionable intelligence.

📄

EOB & Document Intelligence

AI-powered tools that interpret Explanation of Benefits documents, medical coding outputs, and regulatory filings — making complex healthcare paperwork understandable for both operations teams and members.

⚙️

Workflow Automation

Custom AI workflows for healthcare operations — from automated provider credential verification to intelligent routing of appeals and grievances — reducing manual intervention while maintaining compliance.
