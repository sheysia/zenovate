Practices

Three directions, one bench

We work across three practice areas, with the same team rotating in based on the problem rather than handing off across silos.
01

Healthcare Data & AI

Claims analytics, payer operations, EOB and document intelligence, and migrations from legacy data platforms (SQL Server, SAS, on-prem warehouses) to modern cloud-native stacks. We've worked with the messy adjudication logic, edge cases, and compliance requirements that generic AI consultancies tend to miss.

Engagements have included claims datasets at billion-row scale.

02

AI Architecture for Small & Mid-Market Business

Practical AI systems for businesses without enterprise data teams: agents, internal automations, document intelligence, knowledge bases, and lightweight data pipelines. We design for the constraint that one or two people will need to maintain it long after we've handed off.

03

Applied Research & Advisory

Research-grade analysis for product, policy, and regulatory questions. Deliverables include market and policy research, economic modeling, structured white papers, product due diligence, regulatory landscape reviews, and AI-assisted research workflows.
